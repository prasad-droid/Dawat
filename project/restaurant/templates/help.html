Help and Support

How Can We Assist You?

Welcome to Daawat's Help and Support page. We are here to ensure that your experience with us is smooth and enjoyable. If you have any questions or need assistance, please find helpful information below:

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order? You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply select your dishes, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.
  • Can I track my order? Yes, you can track your order in real-time via our tracking feature available on our website or app.
  • What should I do if there’s an issue with my order? If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team immediately using the contact details below.
  • What are your delivery hours? Our delivery hours are from 10 AM to 10 PM daily. Orders can be placed at any time during these hours.

Troubleshooting Guide

  • Issue: Unable to place an order
    Solution: Ensure your payment details are correct and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact support.
  • Issue: Order not delivered on time
    Solution: Check the order tracking status. If there’s a delay, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
  • Issue: Incorrect items received
    Solution: Contact us with your order number and details about the issue. We’ll work to resolve it promptly.
